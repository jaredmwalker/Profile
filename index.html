Jared Walker

I specialize in data. My skills include querying, data management, report programming, data visualization, graphic design, scientific writing, and automation of data processes. I have extensive experience working with others. At Utah State Board of Education, I collaborated with colleagues to develop and administer surveys, clean and organize data, and maintain webpages. As the Data Specialist, I served as the primary or secondary POC for over a dozen routine reports. Additional responsibilities included developing and maintaining written standard operating procedures for routine reports, complying with federal and state regulations, and giving oral presentations in web-based townhall meetings. While working for CNP, it was my privilege to aid in the collection and preparation of data for Pandemic EBT, which was then passed on to Workforce Services for benefit issuance. In late 2022, I left CNP to complete school. Since then, I have worked as a freelance contractor for suddenlysculptedsexy.com and PSG Professional Services.
